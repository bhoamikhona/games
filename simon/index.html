Simon

Score: 6

High Score: 24

Rules

Get ready to watch, remember, repeat! Simon is an exciting game in which players must repeat random sequences of signals by pressing the color buttons in correct order. Experience the fun as you repeat the patterns and advance to higher levels. Keep track of your score as you challenge friends or try to beat your own high score.

  • Press the NEW GAME button. Simon will give the first signal. Repeat the signal by pressing the same color button.
  • Simon will then give the next signal. Repeat the two signals by pressing the same color buttons, in order.
  • Simon will give the next signal and you now have to repeat all the three signals by pressing the same color buttons, in order.
  • Continue playing as long as you can repeat sequence, in order.
  • If you fail to repeat a sequence exactly, you lose.
